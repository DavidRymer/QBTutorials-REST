About us

QB tutorials was founded by David Rymer, a former Maths student with a passion for teaching from the University of York. The purpose of this website is to give students a proper understanding of any topic that may come up in their exams, GCSE or A-Level. The problem with a lot of schools is that they try and teach you how to remember rather than understand, which is an inferior approach to learning. Our tutors are dedicated to helping you understand any topic you want. Our tutorial sessions take place on Skype, which significantly reduces the cost of the sessions for you, the student. We also include free online tests, which is how we will be monitoring your progress and seeing the areas that you are weak in.
